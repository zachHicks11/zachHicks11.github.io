For these kinds of thematic breaks, HTML offers the hr element. The name is short for horizontal rule and the element does just what it says: it draws a horizontal rule (that’s a line to the likes of you and me) clear across the page. The result? A nice, clean visual indication that the next paragraph is in some way a break from the previous one.
